About SDFC Bank

Welcome to SDFC Bank!

SDFC Bank is a modern digital banking application developed using Java, Spring Boot, Spring MVC, Thymeleaf, Spring Data JPA, and MySQL. The application demonstrates real-world banking operations with a simple, secure, and user-friendly interface.

🎯 Our Mission

To provide secure, reliable, and efficient banking services while following modern software engineering practices and enterprise application architecture.

👁 Our Vision

To build a scalable and secure banking platform that simulates real-world banking systems and continuously evolves with advanced enterprise features.

🏦 Banking Services

  • Open New Bank Account
  • Deposit Money
  • Withdraw Money
  • Transfer Money
  • Check Account Balance
  • Search Customer Accounts
  • Close Bank Account
  • View Transaction History
